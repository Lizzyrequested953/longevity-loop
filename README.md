## 🔍 How to Use the Program

The application breaks down aging science into a simple loop. Each step moves you closer to new insights.

### Define Your Question
Open the app and select a new project. State a question about aging. The software helps you frame this as a testable idea. You define what you want to measure.

### Handle Your Data
The software connects to public databases. It pulls in data related to aging clocks and biomarkers. You see the source of every piece of data. This keeps your work honest and open.

### Verify Your Results
The tool uses AI to check your logic. It flags errors in your data. It asks if your results match your original question. This step ensures your findings hold up to review.

### Create Your Report
The system generates a summary of your work. It writes the finding in plain text. You can share this document with others. It tells a story based on data.

### Share and Build
You can post your findings to the community. Other users see your report. They might build on your work. This creates a compounding effect. Your small discovery helps the field grow.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting Issues

If the program fails to start, check the following items.

1. **Check your internet**: The app needs a connection to pull live data.
2. **Update your drivers**: Ensure your graphics drivers meet the requirements.
3. **Run as administrator**: Right-click the icon and choose "Run as administrator" to grant full permissions.
4. **Reinstall**: If the software shows an error message, delete the folder and perform a fresh download from the link at the top.
